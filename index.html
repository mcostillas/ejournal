Places We Visited

Day 1: Departure to Cebu

Davao International Airport (Departure)

Our educational tour began at Francisco Bangoy International Airport (Davao International Airport), where we gathered early in the morning for our flight to Cebu. This modern airport serves as the main gateway to Mindanao and was our starting point for this exciting educational journey.

SM Seaside City Cebu

SM Seaside City Cebu is one of the largest shopping malls in the Philippines, offering a wide range of retail, dining, and entertainment options. The mall features a unique circular design and is situated along the South Road Properties in Cebu City.

Location Map

Magellan's Cross

Magellan's Cross is a Christian cross planted by Portuguese and Spanish explorers as ordered by Ferdinand Magellan upon arriving in Cebu in 1521. It is housed in a small chapel next to the Basilica Minore del Santo Niño on Magallanes Street.

Location Map

Sto. Niño Basilica

The Basilica Minore del Santo Niño is a minor basilica in Cebu City that was founded in 1565. It houses the oldest religious relic in the Philippines - the statue of the Santo Niño (Holy Child Jesus) which was a gift from Ferdinand Magellan to Queen Juana of Cebu in 1521.

Location Map

Fort San Pedro

Fort San Pedro is a military defense structure built by the Spanish under the command of Miguel López de Legazpi. It is the oldest and smallest fort in the Philippines, located in the area now called Plaza Independencia in Cebu City.

Location Map

Castle Peak Hotel Check-in

Castle Peak Hotel is a comfortable accommodation located in the heart of Cebu City. It offers convenient access to many tourist attractions and business centers, making it an ideal base for our educational tour.

Location Map

Day 2: Industry Visits

Alliance Soft Inc.

Alliance Soft Inc. is a leading software development company specializing in custom enterprise solutions, mobile applications, and digital transformation services. During our visit, we learned about their development methodologies, project management approaches, and the latest technologies they implement for their clients.

JCI Cebu

Junior Chamber International (JCI) Cebu is a leadership development organization that provides development opportunities that empower young people to create positive change. Our visit included leadership workshops, networking sessions with local entrepreneurs, and discussions on community development initiatives.

Day 3: Cultural and Scenic Destinations

Go Team

Go Team is an innovative digital solutions company that specializes in web development, mobile applications, and digital marketing strategies. Our visit provided insights into their agile development process, creative design approaches, and how they implement cutting-edge technologies for their diverse clientele.

Cebu Taoist Temple

The Cebu Taoist Temple is a cultural landmark built in 1972 by Cebu's Chinese community. Located in Beverly Hills Subdivision, this temple showcases traditional Chinese architecture with its pagoda-style design, dragon statues, and vibrant colors. Our visit provided insights into Taoist philosophy and Chinese cultural practices in the Philippines.

Temple of Leah

The Temple of Leah, often called "Cebu's Taj Mahal," is a Roman-inspired edifice built by Teodorico Soriano Adarna as a symbol of his love for his late wife, Leah Villa Albino-Adarna. This grand structure features Greek and Roman architectural elements, statues, and an impressive collection of memorabilia. The temple offers panoramic views of Cebu City and serves as a testament to enduring love.

TOPS Cebu

TOPS (The Tops of Cebu) is a popular mountaintop viewpoint offering a breathtaking 360-degree panorama of Cebu City and its surrounding areas. Located approximately 600 meters above sea level in the mountain barangay of Busay, TOPS provides visitors with spectacular views, especially during sunset and at night when the city lights create a mesmerizing display below.

Day 4: Airport Tour and Departure

Mactan Cebu International Airport Tour

Our educational tour included a special behind-the-scenes visit to Mactan Cebu International Airport, known for its award-winning architecture and efficient operations. We learned about airport management, security protocols, and the technical aspects of running an international gateway. The tour provided valuable insights into aviation infrastructure and the tourism industry in the Philippines.

Departure to Davao

After our enriching days in Cebu, we departed for Davao City via a domestic flight from Mactan Cebu International Airport. The journey marked the transition to the next phase of our educational tour. We reflected on our experiences in Cebu while looking forward to exploring Davao's unique cultural and industrial landscape. The flight provided spectacular aerial views of the Philippine archipelago.

Company and Industry Profiles

Alliance Soft Inc.

Alliance Soft Inc. is a leading software development company based in Cebu City, specializing in custom enterprise solutions, mobile applications, and digital transformation services. The company serves clients across various industries including healthcare, finance, and e-commerce.

Technologies Observed:

  • Cloud Infrastructure (AWS, Azure)
  • Full-Stack Development (React, Node.js)
  • Mobile App Development (React Native, Flutter)
  • DevOps & CI/CD Pipelines

Key Innovations:

Alliance Soft has pioneered several innovative approaches to software development, including their proprietary Agile-DevOps integration framework that reduces development cycles by up to 40%. Their AI-powered code review system helps maintain high code quality while accelerating delivery timelines.

JCI Cebu

Junior Chamber International (JCI) Cebu is a leadership development organization that provides development opportunities to empower young people to create positive change. While not a technology company, JCI Cebu plays a crucial role in developing business leadership and entrepreneurial skills in the region.

  • Community Project Management
  • Business Networking Events
